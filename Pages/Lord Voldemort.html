Lord Voldemort



Born Tom Marvolo Riddle, Lord Voldemort or He-Who-Must-Not-be-Named was one of the most powerful Dark wizards of all time. His closest followers, the Death Eaters, were dedicated to establishing a new Voldemort-led supremacy, but were forced into hiding after their leader was mysteriously vanquished when he tried to kill a defenceless infant - a young Harry Potter. Little did anyone know, however, that Voldemort had gone further than any other wizard in his pursuit of immortality, and he would return once more.
